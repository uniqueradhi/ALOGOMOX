Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I browse the products?

Simply visit the homepage or products page to explore our offerings. Use filters or categories for easier navigation.

2. How do I add items to the cart?

Click the "Add to Cart" button under any product to add it to your cart. You can adjust the quantity in the cart section.

3. How do I check out and place an order?

Go to your cart and click "Checkout." Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

4. What should I do if the website isn't loading properly?

Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

How can I contact customer support?

Visit the Contact Us page or email us at support@yourwebsite.com for assistance.
